pitch polishing

Ref : https://www.precision-surface.com/what-is-pitch-polishing.html

Pitch polishing is a similar concept to the lapping process except it is using a pitch plate and much finer polishing media.

  • Pitch


    1. a thick, black, sticky substance that is used on roofs, boats, etc., to keep out water

    2. a sticky substance that is produced by some trees (such as pines)
