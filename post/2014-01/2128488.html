dynamic viscosity (mu) : 
[SI unit] Pa.sec
[공학단위] P (poise) = 0.1 Pa.sec

kinematic viscosity (nu = mu/density)
[SI unit] m2/sec
[공학단위] St (stroke) = 1 cm2/sec = 10^-4 m2/sec


물의 dynamic viscosity
1.0050 cP at 20 degC
0.4061 cP at 70 degC

물의 kinematic viscosity
1.007E-6 m2/sec = 1.007 cSt at 20 degC
0.556E-6 m2/sec = 0.556 cSt at 50 degC

공기의 kinematic viscosity
1.515E-5 m2/sec = 15.15 cSt at 20 degC
1.785E-5 m2/sec = 17.85 cSt at 50 degC
