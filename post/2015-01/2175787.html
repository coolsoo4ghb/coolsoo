A substance of very high concern (SVHC) is a chemical substance (or part of a group of chemical 
substances) for which it has been proposed that the use within the European Union be subject to
 authorisation under the REACH Regulation

REACH
사용하는 물질(Substance)의 양, 위해성 등에 따라 등록, 평가, 허가를받아야 하는 EU의‘新 화학물질관리제도’
