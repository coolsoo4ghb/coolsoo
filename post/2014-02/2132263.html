Fomblin® PFPE lubricants Galden® PFPE solvent fluids are composed of fluorine, carbon and oxygen (known as PFPE or perfluoropolyether)

Fomblin® PFPE lubricants are formulated in many variants by adding thickeners, solvents, and additives that effectively protect moving parts from corrosion, wear, and extreme loads and are engineered for applications where heat, chemicals, solvents, corrosion, toxicity, flammability, compatibility, and service life present lubrication problems.

In sealed-for-life applications, Fomblin® PFPE lubricants  offer virtual lifetime lubrication and they don’t carbonize or deteriorate at high temperatures.

Fomblin® PFPE lubricants Galden® PFPE solvent fluids have an indefinite shelf life when stored unopened in a clean, dry location.

Galden® PFPE solvent fluids are high performance , inert liquids showing the highest boiling points among fluorinated heat transfer fluids, no flash or fire point, no auto-ignition point, safe-to-use and developed for applications where chemical and thermal stability coupled with excellent thermal properties are required.

Both Fomblin® PFPE lubricants and Galden® PFPE solvent fluids feature:

  • Unmatched chemical and solvent resistance
  • Non-reactive with metals, plastics and elastomers
  • Chemically and thermally stable
  • Non-Flam, Non-toxic, No Auto-Ignition Point
  • Safe to use (FM approved 6930 standard)
  • Wide operating temperature range
  • No flash or fire point, no auto-ignition point
  • Highest boiling point among fluorinated fluids
  • Good dielectric properties
  • Odourless and colourless appearance
