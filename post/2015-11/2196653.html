RS-3 is a modified polycyanate resin developed to provide a good balance between toughness 
and high temperature/wet performance. RS-3 has been evaluated and qualified in the areas of 
satellite, airframe/missile, and dielectric structures. RS-3 is also available in several modified 
formulations including: RS-3BH, an electrostatic dissipation version; RS-3C, a controlled flow 
vacuum bag/oven cure version; and RS-3M, an extremely low-flow autoclave version

RS-12 is a low temperature curing modified polycyanate resin, for aerospace and radome applications. RS-12 has been evaluated and qualified in the areas of satellite and dielectric structures. 
RS-12 is also available in modified formulations: RS-12B, a low-tack, controlled flow version; and 
RS-12D, a low-tack, extremely low-flow autoclave version.
